Looking for Presenters

Get your proposals in by {{ callForPapers.due|date('F jS, Y') }}!

Welcome to the DE DevCon Call for Papers! The CFP will close on {{ callForPapers.due|date('F jS, Y h:i:s A') }} EST. Please make sure that you get your submissions in before that time! No extensions will be granted.

What We Look For

We are looking for a variety of talks on work-related subjects. Talks should be 50 minutes in length. We want a wide variety of topics, including but not limited to DevOps, Big Data, and Agile Development.

What to expect

When the Call for Papers closes, the organizers will sort through the submissions, making their selections. Speakers will be notified no later than {{ callForPapers.selection|date('F jS, Y') }}. The goal is to give our speakers at least a month to prepare their presentations.

Suggestions to Get Accepted

We strongly encourage potential speakers to submit more than one submission on more than one topic. The more submissions you contribute, the more options you give us to choose you as a speaker. Be sure your talk title and abstract define the exact topic you want to speak about and what you hope people will learn from the session.

Talk Types

Tutorials

These will take place on Thursday and are 3 hours long. Attendees here will expect in-depth coverage of a specific topic.

Regular talks

These take place throughout the rest of the conference and are 50 minutes long, including the Q & A.
